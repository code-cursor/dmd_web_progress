About Duchenne Muscular Dystrophy (DMD)

Duchenne Muscular Dystrophy (DMD) is a severe genetic condition characterized by progressive muscle weakening due to the absence of dystrophin, a key protein that helps keep muscle cells intact. It typically affects young boys and leads to gradual loss of mobility and strength.
