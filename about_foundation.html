We help children affected by Muscular Dystrophy

DMD Care Foundation Bangladesh is a nonprofit initiative founded by parents of children affected by Duchenne Muscular Dystrophy (DMD) and Becker Muscular Dystrophy (BMD). Our mission is to raise awareness, support families, and improve access to therapies, assistive devices, and education.

  • Raise awareness about DMD/BMD
  • Build a national patient registry
  • Facilitate medical and therapeutic support
  • Advocate for policy inclusion and collaboration
  • Support education and inclusive opportunities

Every child with Duchenne or Becker Muscular Dystrophy receives early diagnosis, proper care, and the opportunity to live with dignity, inclusion, and hope.

  • Team formation by parents and volunteers
  • Ongoing awareness campaigns
  • Documentation of 100+ children
  • Foundation setup in progress
  • Planning collaborations with global DMD networks
